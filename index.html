About Us

Shiloh Hospital, located at 17, Ajokakan Avenue, Elebu, Oluyole Extension, Ibadan, Nigeria, is dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services to our community.

As a general-purpose hospital, we specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of a wide range of ailments, ensuring comprehensive care for patients of all ages.

Our team of experienced medical professionals is committed to delivering personalized, compassionate care with a focus on promoting health and wellness.

Equipped with modern facilities and a patient-centered approach, Shiloh Hospital strives to be a trusted partner in your healthcare journey, offering reliable and affordable medical services to meet your everyday health needs.

Our Vision

At Shiloh Hospital, our vision is to be the leading healthcare provider in Nigeria, recognized for our unwavering commitment to excellence in patient care. We aim to create a healthier community by delivering comprehensive, accessible, and affordable medical services. Through innovation, compassion, and a dedication to continuous improvement, we strive to set the standard for quality healthcare, fostering trust and confidence in every patient we serve.

Our Services

At Shiloh Hospital, we are dedicated to providing a wide range of healthcare services designed to meet the needs of our patients. Our core services include:

  • General Consultation: Comprehensive medical assessments for accurate diagnosis and personalized treatment plans.
  • Emergency Care: 24/7 emergency services to handle urgent medical situations with speed and precision.
  • Pediatrics: Specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents, ensuring their growth and well-being.
  • Diagnostic Services: State-of-the-art laboratory tests and imaging services for precise health evaluations.
  • Maternity Care: Compassionate support for expectant mothers, from prenatal care to safe deliveries.

Our goal is to deliver quality, accessible, and affordable healthcare services to ensure your overall well-being.
